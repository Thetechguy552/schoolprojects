WELCOME


Hi, I am Chris Kelly owner of Light Electric.
My focus is customer service, safe and quality
workmanship. I graduated from Sault College in
1993 as an Electronics Engineering Technician
and started my career shortly after that. After
completing my apprenticeship, I quickly moved
into a customer service roll, installing and
repairing all types of electrical devices
and equipment. I still had a thirst for knowledge, so
I studied and received my CFAA Fire Alarm
Technician and Master Electrician licenses. I
started Light Electric in 2012 where I have
gained a great reputation with clients and ESA
around Sault Ste. Marie. I take great pride in my
work and look forward to meeting new clients.

Cheers,
Chris

  • I.A.P.A
  • HILTI training
  • Construction Safety Association of Ontario Training
  • Electrical Construction and Maintenance Levels 1&2
  • PUC & Great Lakes Power Contractor Safety Orientation
  • Man lift training
  • Fall arrest training
  • SAFE Communities Safety Orientation
